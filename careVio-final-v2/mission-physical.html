Mission Physicals

Before Scheduling a Mission Physical:

  • Obtain TB test results - We don’t conduct TB tests in the office. You can obtain TB testing at Community Wellness and Safety - 522 N. Gilbert Rd. #104 Gilbert, AZ 85234. TB tests are $30 to give and read. They are open to walk-ins from 8 am-6 pm Monday-Friday. You can also obtain these at selected Walgreens, CVS or another clinic of your choice. ​
  • Obtain any necessary specialty vaccines - We do offer these vaccines TdaP, Td, pneumonia, meningitis (ACWY) and flu vaccinations in the office.

What to Bring to Your Doctors Appointment:

  • Medical forms from the mission portal
  • Your complete vaccination list
  • Full list of any medications including dosage
  • TB testing results
  • Any other relevant medical history
  • Be hydrated at your appointment so we can draw blood and take a urine sample in the office.

Finalizing Paperwork:

  • You will leave your paperwork in our office until we obtain lab results
  • Once we receive lab results we will complete and sign the paperwork and alert you when they are ready.
