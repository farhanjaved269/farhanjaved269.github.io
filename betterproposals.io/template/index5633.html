Let's start with Your New Website

For over two years in the business, Innovitive Technologies designed multiple websites similar to yours.  

To get started, we would like to point out several components that have to be developed to benefit your website and achieve the goals you're hoping for:

Home Page

A solid call to action page which will feature your most prestigious projects and important categories and a attracting email subscribers from which a slow burn relationship will be formed.

About Us

We suggest taking new team photos and action shots of people working.

These are great and far better than stock photos which unfortunately litter your industry.

Case Studies

Listing clients is great, testimonials are better but giving it some context with a case study is amazing.

Adding a range of case studies to your website will serve you for years to come.

Our Services

The best way to add them to your website is by splitting them into sections named after your main services and adding the matching headers. The headers we had in mind were the following: 'Bookkeeping', 'Saving Tax', and 'Audits'. By doing this, potential clients will search for solutions to their problems in a fast and effective manner.

Call to Action

We will strategically place a contact form at the bottom of each page, offering the possibility to email any question a visitor may have in the process.

Process

What exactly working with 4 WALLS looks like?

Our Process

What to expect working with us

At Innovitive Technologies, we offer a comprehensive approach to web development. We look at your brand and business, your values and ethos AND your target audience, in order to deliver a web solution that not only visually engages, but ensures you achieve your business goals.

To keep things simple, and everyone collaborating effectively, we find this is best achieved in 5 Simple Steps:

1 - Design Interview

We need to determine who you are, what your brand voice is and what your market space is looking for.

Think of this as a "two-way" research drive, to determine not only how best to represent your value, but to explore the values your core audience is looking for and help you resonate with them.

2 - Design Presentation

Like choice? We will deliver three design concepts for you to select from.

Naturally, there will be a common theme, but we strive to think outside of the box and come up with different solutions for you to review and discuss with us. This brainstorming and comparison part is often where the best ideas come from! Once a clear option has been chosen, we will move forward with any adjustments you have noted.

3 - Website Content

Now that we have your design decided on, we can begin to construct the website. Our copywriter will be in touch with you to help put your ideas down through the "voice" of your new brand. 

Any photos attempted to use on the website will have to be gathered at this stage.

4 - Website Build

Using everything we have gathered, we will start building your website. Once it is done, we will present it to you and go through the details and finishing touches following the objectives we have originally set.

Any amends are made at this stage.

Step 5 - Launch

The launch process has to be carefully considered. We need to ensure email addresses will not go down, the site doesn't have any downtime, any old links are re-directed to the new pages, as well as several other things. 

The following process has been done by our company many times before, and we have developed a detailed checklist we are working with, that has not failed us once.

We make sure to launch the website late on Friday's, as that gives us a chance to iron out any possible issues.  You can sleep well knowing we will be on-call over your launch weekend to ensure everything goes according to plan.
